Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

This project analyzes 3,900 retail transactions to understand customer shopping behavior, spending patterns, and subscription tendencies.

Project Overview

   ● Transaction-level data across multiple product categories

   ● Focus on demographics, discounts, shipping, ratings, and subscriptions

   ● Goal: generate insights to support marketing, loyalty, and pricing decisions

Tech Stack

   ● Python (pandas, NumPy) for data cleaning, EDA, and feature engineering

   ● MySQL for business queries and aggregations

   ● Power BI for interactive dashboarding and KPI visualization

Key Steps

   1. Data Preparation

        ● Handle missing review ratings (median by category)

        ● Standardize column names and formats

        ● Engineer features: age_group, purchase_frequency_days

   2. SQL Analysis

       ● Revenue by gender and age group

       ● Discount impact on high spenders

       ● Subscribers vs non-subscribers

       ● Customer segmentation: New, Returning, Loyal

       ● Top products by rating and by category

   3. Dashboard

       ● KPIs: revenue, average purchase amount, average rating

       ● Views for revenue by category, customer segment, age group, and shipping type

Business Insights (Examples)

   ● Identify high-value customer segments for targeted campaigns

   ● Evaluate discount dependency and subscription impact on revenue

   ● Highlight top-rated and best-selling products for promotion
